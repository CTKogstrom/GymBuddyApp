We are a group of College Students studying Computer Science at the University of Florida. We all share a strong passion for both mental and physical health/fitness, as well as helping others upgrade and become the best versions of themselves.

In the past, we have all shared similar struggles in finding a coherent application to track various aspects of our health. Therefore, we decided to come together and create an "all in one" application that tracks bodyweight, lifts, macros, and calories, suggest workout plans and caloric intake per day, and more all personalized to the individual!
